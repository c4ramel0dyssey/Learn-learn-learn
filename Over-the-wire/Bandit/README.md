# Bandit

Soo Bandit is one of the wargames offered by OverTheWire community, spesifically designed to help players
grow familiarity with Linux basic commands.  

Challenges are given for each level and in order to move to the next, players have to work their way in finding the passwords
hidden in one of the files. Instructions, clues, and website links for additional reading are given for each stage.  

Though I've already learned most of them through my participations in CTF, I consider this a good opportunity to practice what
I've known and also pickup on what's new. Idk if I'll solve all the way until the very last level tho. We'll see.
